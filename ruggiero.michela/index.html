My vision

My stories, my ideas

  • Setpember 2013

    Marble Eco Design

    A research project: the main goal is to develop a new 3D printing technique, exploiting debris material resulting from common marble cave operations, which would require additional costs for discarding. Such powder is mixed with a light-sensitive polymer and fed from a custom-made 3D printer.

  • January 2014

    Fab Lab Frosinone

    The first digital fabrication center in Ciociaria. Through 3d printing, milling and laser cutting processes, Fab Lab Officine Giardino experiments new production strategies and supports Innovative thinking in it's community

  • June 2014

    Henka

    A flexible, fast-moving, eco-friendly and eco-save project. A product which changes in shape and colour, a combination of modular self-enlightening elements deriving from the reuse of marble dust. Henka project won Reshape, digital craft online competition

  • October 2014

    Roland Hub

    Roland Hub is created to stimulate and encourage the creation of new businesses, and to give a new impulse and new perspectives to realities already active. Workshops, training and design, to share the experience of creative contamination, to accelerate the business with new innovative projects
