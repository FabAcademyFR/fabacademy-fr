THE CONCEPT

Final Project

Building Thinkers

I am an architect, with the relationship with my clients, I can understand the growing need to design with customization, the continuing dissatisfaction of people, and the constant need for change.


For this I thought the boxes! Everything is a boxs, the home, the public places, the workplaces.



The question is: how do I make a box dynamic?

I explode the box and have the walls.


This is why I decided to design an interactive panel, to simulate a wall that changes.


I choose to think of it in a museum.


The mechanical system is similar to the gear of a bicycle.



The closure system of the holes is similar to the objective of a camera.



For this I thought the boxes! Everything is a boxs, the home, the public places, the workplaces.
