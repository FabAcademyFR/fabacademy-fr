THE CONCEPT

Final Project

Building Thinkers

I am an architect and a designer, and dealing with my customers, I realized how computerization profoundly impacted all aspects of design. It has not only transformed design processes and design economics, it has transformed the nature of the products that can be created. I always deal with a growing need for customization, a continuing dissatisfaction of people, and a constant demand for change.


For this reason I thought of the boxes! Everything is a box, homes, public places, workplaces ...



The question is: how to make a dynamic box?

I exploded a box and I got walls.


I decided to design an interactive panel, to simulate a wall that changes shape and function.


I choose to figure a wall in a museum.


The system is similar to the mechanics of a bicycle, with gears and toothed belt.



I thought of an opening/closing system like the diaphragm of a camera.
