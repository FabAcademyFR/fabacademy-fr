Applications and Implications

Applications and Implications about my final project

Assignment: propose a final project that integrates the range of units covered:

WHAT WILL IT DO?
- The project I want to develop is an interactive panel. It opens and closes responding to pulses derived from environmental conditions, creating an artistic and functional effect.

WHAT MATERIALS AND COMPONENTS WILL BE REQUIRED?

Mechanical components:
- 4 threaded rods
- 24 bolts
- toothed belt 10€
- 3 plywood panels 1mx1m - 20€
- Stretch fabric
- 5 pulleys
- 1 Wiper Motor
- 1 Power module

Electronic components:
- Fab Kit Miky
- 2 Electronic limit 3€
- 2 Button 4€
- 1 Phototransistor 3,5€
- 1 Temperature sensor 3€

WHERE WILL THEY COME FROM?
I'll realize mechanical parts with 3D printer and laser cut. Raw materials such as PLA are produced in Puglia, an Italian region. The plywood comes from a carpenter of Frosinone, the toothed belt from China, the elastic tissue is produced by a factory in Frosinone. The windscreen wiper engine comes from a wrecking yard, and the power module from an old laptop. All electronic components, come from online shop, mainly from China.

HOW MUCH WILL IT COST?
It will cost about 70 euro.

WHAY PARTS AND SYSTEMS WILL BE MADE?
I will produce by myself all electronic shields, davits/hoist, and plywood panels.

WHAT PROCESSES WILL BE USED?
The electronic shields will be designed and milled with the SRM20. The Davits / hoist will be modeled and 3D printed, and the panels will be laser cutted.

WHAT TASKS NEED TO BE COMPLETED?
Both the process of realization of the parts and their fitting need to be completed. As regards electronic components, Ill' wait the arrival of components.

WHAT QUESTIONS NEED TO BE ANSWERED?
- How can I control the behavior of mechanical and electronic components?
- It will be a useful project?
- Will it work?

WHAT IS THE SCHEDULE?
- I'll start printing components with 3D printer;
- After that, I'll check the correct dimensions of the joints and consequently I'll start to cut the plywood;
- When electronic components will be here, I'll weld, and I'll make Arduino sketch.

HOW WILL IT BE EVALUATED?
The project will be evaluated by working or less of the interactive wall, but also according with his cheapness, simplicity and functionality.
