Week 12

Composites

COMPOSITES MATERIALS

Assignment: Design and make a 3D mold (~ft2), and produce a fiber composite part in it.
For this exercise, I decided to create a clutch.

DESIGN
- To design the 3D object I used Rhino.

3D MODEL:

Rhino: Make sure it is in the box -x + y axis with the origin point 0.0. Export the model as Stl.

MILLING:
- Enterprise

MATERIALS:
- Polyurethane.

TOOLS:
-Endmill 8 mm
-Ballmill 3.2 mm
-Mask
-Screws
-Glasses
-Screwdriver
-Gloves
-Headphones
-Vacuum cleaner
-Motion sensors


ROUGHING:
- For roughing i used endmill = 8 mm

FINISHING:
- For finishing i used ballmill = 3.2 mm


CLEANING:
Apply the release agent to assist the removal of the silicone.

SMOOTHING:
Calibrate the container and does the right quantities of the two components. Silicone Part-A, white color, and Silicon Part-B, red color. Parts A & B are mixed 100A:3B by weight. Pot life is 40 minutes and cure time is 24 hours.


WATERPROOFING:
Mix thoroughly until the material is not homogeneous.

During the pouring of the silicone into the mold, proceed very gently.
We must ensure, to distribute silicone everywhere, trying not to create air bubbles.

WAXING:
After 24 hours




The work is in progress because processing time is long. The object needs 12 hours of vacuum. Tomorrow we will discard the package.
