DIGITAL PROCESS TO CREATE CLOTHES

To create the garment for the project I can follow the so-called Digital Tailoring Process through the following steps:

1-Body Scan

2-Digital Draping

3-Digital Patterning

4-Costruction

5-Print Cut and Stitch

This process help me to create clothes designed and patterned specifically for a determinate body avoiding measurements and sizes.
