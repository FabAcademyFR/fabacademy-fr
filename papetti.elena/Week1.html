During the first week of the Fab Academy we were asked to make a personal webpage and present an idea for our final projects. First, I had to learn html5, for documentation and follow-up purposes. FINAL PROJECT PROPOSAL:

The idea of my Final Project is a wearable controller system to help monitoring the right posture during sports activities. Monitoring will take place through sensors that notify the error with a vibration when the body is out of position. So it would be possible improve physical performance and take care of health avoiding chronics pains or long-term damages.

DIGITAL PROCESS TO CREATE CLOTHES

To create the garment for the project I can follow the so-called Digital Tailoring Process through the following steps:

1-Body Scan

2-Digital Draping

3-Digital Patterning

4-Costruction

5-Print Cut and Stitch

This process help me to create clothes designed and patterned specifically for a determinate body avoiding measurements and sizes.
