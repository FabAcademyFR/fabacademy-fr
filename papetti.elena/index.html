Elena Papetti

  • Short bio

    Born in Frosinone -Italy- in 1984, Elena is an italian Architect. She studied at "La Sapienza" Università di Roma. She's specialized in Exhibition and Interior Design. Her works are focused on the recovery existing buildings with particular attention to industrial ones. Through the Frosinone's Fab Lab she came in contact with the maker community and now she's intentioned to utilize the Fab Academy to learn and understand elecrtonic and fabrication technologies.
