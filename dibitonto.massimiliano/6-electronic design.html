6 - Electronics Design

Intro

The assignment of this week was to modify the Hello Board adding a led and a button.

Modifying the HelloBoard

Even if I'm quite familiar with the basics of electronics I've never designed a pcd. Usually I make my circuits using breadboards (helpful to learn how stuff works) and stripboards. I installed Eagle for mac and started designing the circuit. I started from the scheme, adding a button with a pulldown resistor (10k ohm) and a led with a resistor (500 ohm). The fun part was to find enough space I wasn't able to find a simulator for Eagle neither a Spice version working on mac. I played with 123D Circuits simulator and with Fritzing. To mill my board I selected only the first layer, I set the resolution @600 dpi and I exported the image. I created also another file to cut the boundaries of the board. I used fabmodules to create the gcode files for the Roland. I decided to add an "M" on the board to make it more personal :)
