    The Fabduck projects, including code, electronic boards and 3D models is meant to build a social robots designed to live inside a pool.
    It reads some data from sensor mounted on an Arduino compatible board and shares the data on the web in different modalities. You can find a detailed description here http://fabacademy.org/archives/2015/eu/students/dibitonto.massimiliano/final_project.html
