About Me

I’m an interaction designer and a user experience researcher living in Rome. I’m a phD in Computer Science, I hold a M.S. in Mass Communication and New Media and a B.A. in Communication Sciences and Technologies. My main research interests are in Human Computer Interaction, Interaction Design, User Experience and Physical Computing. My background is both humanistic and technical, and my main skills stem from my ability of merging these two worlds. I’m fascinated by natural interaction paradigms and new ways to create interactions between the user and the technology.
